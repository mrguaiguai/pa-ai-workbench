# Policy Analysis Skill

## Purpose

Turn policy material into structured analysis for operational review.

## Inputs

- `query_or_topic`: policy topic or analysis request.
- `business_area`: optional domain filter.
- `document_type`: optional policy document type.
- `document_ids`: optional document scope.
- `extra_requirements`: optional analysis focus.

## Evidence Rules

- Ground conclusions in retrieved policy evidence.
- Separate confirmed requirements from inferred implications.
- Highlight missing evidence or conflicting evidence.
- Avoid storing or revealing sensitive full text.

## Output

- Executive summary.
- Key requirements or changes.
- Impact and risk points.
- Recommended follow-up actions.
- Citations and warnings.
